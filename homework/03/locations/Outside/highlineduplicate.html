THE HIGHLINE

Our first standalone scoop shop in Manhattan, our Chelsea scoop shop opened in the fall of 2018.
It was also our first scoop shop to host Ice Cream Happy Hour! Every Wednesday from 5pm to 7pm, we make
it easy to indulge your sweet tooth by offering happy hour prices on our ice cream. Smack-dab in the middle
of the work week, Ice Cream Happy Hour gives you an excuse to take that much-needed break. Our Chelsea
scoop shop is also the only shop to offer deluxe specialty cones, made in house, right there in front
of you (imagine that fresh waffle cone smell wafting through the shop). It's a bit of a trial right now;
if you love them, let us know! We're thinking about offering them everywhere. Click image to learn more!
